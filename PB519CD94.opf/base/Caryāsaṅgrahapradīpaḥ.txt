caryāsaṅgrahapradīpaḥ|namo lokeśvarāyamadādyaśeṣamūḍhānāṃ yasya vacanaraśmibhiḥ|phullatāmeti
hṛtpadmaṃ taṃ vande puruṣottamam

pāramiṃ guhyamantraṃ ca śritvā bodhiḥ prasidhyati|gurubuddhoktitastvevaṃ tadartho likhyate mayā

guhyamantro'tra no vācyaḥ pāramīnayacārikāḥ|bodhisattvasya caryāstu samāsena likhāmyaham

dhīmataḥ saṃvaro grāhyo bodhicittapuraḥsaraḥ|ālokyāśeṣasūtrāṇi śāstraṃ śravyaṃ samastakam

kāyena manasā vācā yathoktān saṃvarān tribhiḥ|rakṣedakṣuṇṇaśuddhāṃśca śīlāṃśca pariśodhayet

bhaktamātrāṃ vijānīyād indriyadvāramāvaret|rātreḥ pūrve'pare bhāge na suptvā yogamācaret

aṇumātreṣvavadyeṣu hyatimātraṃ bibheti ca|rātrerbhāgatrayaṃ kṛtvā hyantye bhāge tu jāgṛyāt

prakṣālayenmukhādīn vā'kṣālanaṃ vā'pi yujyate|sukhāsanaṃ samāśritya dharmāṇāṃ dharmatāṃ smaret

nimittaiḥ kṣobhato'śakye tūtthāyābhāsavastuṣu|māyāvattvena samprekṣya puṇyayogāṃstadantare

pūrayeccāpi saptāṅgān vipulāṃ praṇidhiṃ caret|bhāvayed bhāvanāṃ pūrvāṃ kāle'tha bhojanasya ca

etanniḥsārakāyena paraṃ sāraṃ gaveṣayan|kāyaṃ naukādhiyā rakṣed na bhuṅktāṃ sthūlatākṛte

rasāsaktyā na bhuñjīta caturdhā'nnaṃ vibhajya ca|devebhyo vinivedyādiṃ dharmapālāya tatparam

baliṃ suvipulāṃ dadyāt śeṣaṃ svabhuktapītataḥ|dadyācca sarvabhūtebhyaḥ kathātantraṃ tadantare

kuryādadbhutavārttā ca kiñcidutthāya sañcaret|parikrāmetadadhiṣṭhānaṃ japaṃ vā granthavācanam

sugatapratimāṃ kuryād yāvat svedo na jāyate|kuryāt, pradakṣiṇāṃ tāvad akṣubdhaḥ praṇidhiṃ bahum

dharmacaryā daśaproktāḥ maitreyeṇa, samāsataḥ|caredakṣiptacittena māyaupamyaṃ ca saṃsmaran

yadi syāt pūjayet saṃghaṃ kuryād vā bālakotsavam|anāthebhyaḥ sudānaṃ tu yogine puṇyasañcayaḥ

pūrṇeṣu dinakṛtyeṣu bhāge ca prathame niśaḥ|dharmatā niṣprapañcā'pi tathā cittaṃ ca yojayet

prāptāyāṃ madhyarātrau ca utthānābhāsasaṃjñayā|siṃhanidrā yathā tadvat śubhanidrāṃ samāśrayet

prāyo dhyāna-dṛḍhe citte kāyavākpuṇyagauṇatā|asaṃspṛṣṭe samādhau vā lokakalpapravṛttaye

kāyapuṇyaṃ yathāśakti lokacitte'same sati|dharmo nāyaṃ mamaiveti susadāśayapūrvakam

dharmāṃśca laukikān pṛcched, nijamitrasamīritaḥ|nepālaviṣaye kṛtavān, ratirmantranaye na ced

evaṃ sthavira! karttavyam| 23, a|‘caryāsaṅgrahapradīpo’ mahāpaṇḍitācārya-dīpaṅkara-śrījñānakṛtaḥ
samāptaḥ|
